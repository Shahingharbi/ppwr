# Pactum — SEO Strategy (FR + EN)

Last updated: 2026-04-30. Owner: Pactum Editorial. Document is living — revise quarterly.

## 1. Executive summary

Pactum is launching a bilingual PPWR consulting site (`pactum-advisory.eu`) on the back of a regulatory milestone — Regulation (EU) 2025/40 applies on **12 August 2026**. The niche is genuinely under-served: a SEMrush sweep shows fewer than 10 sites in EU markets indexing seriously on PPWR core terms, and most are trade-press articles, not commercial offers. The 6-month plan: dominate FR keywords on the back of low difficulty (KD < 30) and the 2026 deadline pressure, then expand to DE (largest absolute volume — `ppwr verordnung` 1.3K/mo) by month 3, IT/ES/NL by month 6, Nordics by month 12. North-star metric: combined organic sessions on `/fr/*` and `/en/*`. Leading indicator: 26 indexed routes per locale within 4 weeks. Lagging: 30+ inbound leads/month on `/contact` by Q3.

## 4. On-page SEO checklist

For every locale variant of every page:

- **Title** ≤ 60 chars, format `[Page-specific] | Pactum`. Primary KW in first 50 chars.
- **Meta description** 150-160 chars, verb-led, contains primary KW + a number/date.
- **H1** unique, contains primary KW or close variant. One per page.
- **H2/H3** include 2-3 secondary KWs naturally. Don't repeat the same H2 across pages.
- **Internal links** ≥ 3 per page to related services / sectors / resources.
- **Outbound links** ≥ 1 to authoritative source (EUR-Lex, European Commission, EUROPEN).
- **Image alts** descriptive of regulatory or material concept.
- **Schema.org JSON-LD**: Organization (site-wide), ProfessionalService, BreadcrumbList per page, Service + FAQPage on services, Article + BreadcrumbList on blog, DefinedTermSet on glossary, FAQPage on FAQ pillar.
- **Canonical** URL set per locale, hreflang `fr` / `en` / `x-default=fr`.
- **OpenGraph** + Twitter card per locale (`fr_FR` / `en_GB`).
- **Page weight** < 200kB on first paint, fonts preloaded woff2.

## 7. Backlink and authority strategy

### Tier 1 — earned media (top priority)
Trade publications: Packaging Europe, Packaging Insights, Packaging Digest, Recycling International, Resource Magazine, Sustainable Packaging News, Plastic News Europe, Emballages Magazine (FR), Plastiques & Caoutchoucs (FR), CartonWelt (DE), Verpackungs-Rundschau (DE).

Approach: pitch original research, not generic op-eds. Example pitch: "Pactum analysis of 184 SKUs across European FMCG: 38% will fail Class A recyclability under PPWR Article 6 by 2030." Each pitch ties to a Pactum-hosted resource with embed-friendly chart.

### Tier 2 — owned cadence
- LinkedIn: founder posts 2× / week + Pactum company page 1× / week.
- Newsletter 1× / month.
- Guest articles: 3 per month across the publications above.
- Webinar partnerships: EUROPEN, EuRIC, ECOS, Citeo, Plastics Europe — co-host quarterly.

### Tier 3 — directory + citation
- Directories: clutch.co, Gartner Peer Insights, GoodFirms, AdvisoryHQ.
- Wikipedia: contribute neutral, well-cited edits to the PPWR article. Donate primary research; do not link-spam.
- Reddit: r/sustainability, r/europeanunion, r/ManufacturingPorn — answer compliance questions; link only when directly useful.

### Digital PR hooks
- Quarterly "PPWR Readiness Index" — survey 50 brand owners.
- Free DoC template (gated, GDPR-clean).
- Free recyclability A/B/C self-test (already at `/resources/ppwr-readiness-assessment`).

## 11. Risks and mitigation

- **Algorithm updates**: maintain E-E-A-T signals (named author, citations, dates, regulation references). Avoid AI content that reads generated.
- **AI overviews / SGE**: counter with deep, specific content (article numbers, exact thresholds) — precisely the data points AI overviews like to cite.
- **Search volume reality**: PPWR searches are still climbing — volumes here are floors. Expect 2-3× growth as Aug 2026 approaches.
- **Regulatory delays**: refresh affected articles within 7 days. Keep an internal regulatory tracker.
- **Competitor entry (Big-4)**: counter via specialism depth (article-by-article granularity) and lead magnets they will not produce.

### French regulatory writing style

- "vous" form throughout.
- "Le Règlement", "la Commission", "les États membres" capitalised when referring to EU institutions.
- Dates: "12 août 2026", "1er janvier 2030".
- Percentages: "30 %" (non-breaking space).
- Numbers ≥ 4 digits: thin space "1 234".
- "PPWR" uppercase always; "Règlement (UE) 2025/40" first mention then "le Règlement" or "la PPWR" thereafter.
- Avoid anglicisms ("metteur sur le marché" not "brand owner"; "feuille de route" not "roadmap").

### Editorial QC: every article before publish

- Article numbers verified against Reg. (EU) 2025/40 (CELEX 32025R0040).
- Dates verified against EUR-Lex.
- Percentages and thresholds verified.
- Outbound link to EUR-Lex on first article reference.
- Internal link to ≥ 1 service page and ≥ 1 resource page.
- TipBox count: 2-4. Tables: 1-3. Word count: ≥ 1 500 (article), ≥ 2 500 (pillar).
- Schema.org JSON-LD validated via Rich Results Test.
